As a reminder: Press the F key to answer FALSE, or the J key to answer TRUE.

Please keep your left finger on the F-key, and your right finger on the J-key, as well as you thumbs on the spacebar throughout the experiment.

Before the Experiment begins, you will go through some practice sentences in order to get the hang of the task.

After this, we will begin the actual experiment, which should last between 5 and 10 minutes.

As soon as you click on "start the experiment", the first practice trial will begin. You will read "Wait for nextr trial..." on the screen and three seconds later you will see the sentence to be evaluated, presented word-by-word.
